How to Use EAC Lost & Found

📢

Report a Lost Item

  1. Click "Report Lost Item" on the homepage.
  2. Fill in the item details: title, category, description, location, and date lost.
  3. Upload a photo of the item if available — this helps others identify it.
  4. Provide your contact information so finders can reach you.
  5. Submit the report. An admin will review and approve it before it appears on the site.
  6. Once approved, your item will show under the Lost Items tab.
🔍

Report a Found Item

  1. Click "Report Found Item" on the homepage.
  2. Describe the item: title, category, description, location, and date found.
  3. Upload a photo so the owner can recognize their item.
  4. Indicate whether the item has been surrendered to OSA.
  5. Submit the report. An admin will review and approve it before it goes live.
  6. Once approved, it will appear under the Found Items tab for others to claim.
✅

Claim an Item

  1. Browse the Found Items tab and click "Claim This Item" on the matching item.
  2. Fill out the claim form with a description of your proof of ownership.
  3. The admin or finder will review and approve your claim.
  4. If the item is stored with OSA, visit the Office of Student Affairs with a valid ID.
  5. Present your proof of ownership, sign the release logbook, and collect your item.
For questions, contact OSA at (046) 416-4341 or visit the office directly.

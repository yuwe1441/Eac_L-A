How to Claim Items in OSA

  1. Go to the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) at EAC Cavite during office hours.
  2. Present a valid school ID or government-issued ID for verification.
  3. Provide a detailed description of the lost item and any proof of ownership (if available).
  4. Fill out the claim form provided by OSA staff.
  5. Wait for the staff to verify your claim. If approved, sign the release logbook.
  6. Receive your item and keep your claim receipt for future reference.
For questions, contact OSA at (046) 416-4341 or visit the office directly.
